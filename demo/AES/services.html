Warehouse Standup

A successful warehouse standup involves a holistic approach that addresses physical infrastructure, technology, safety, and human factors to create a well-functioning and efficient operational environment.

  1. Needs Assessment:
    • Determine the specific functions and processes that the warehouse must support.
  2. Infrastructure:
    • Implement and route the proper electrical infrastructure to meet the warehouse needs.
    • Install proper lighting to meet safety standards and operational needs.
  3. Racking and Storage Systems:
    • Design and install racking systems to optimize vertical and horizontal storage space.
  4. Material Handling Equipment:
    • Acquire and install forklifts, pallet jacks, and other material handling equipment.
  5. Safety Measures:
    • Install safety features such as guardrails, bollards, and safety barriers to protect workers and assets.
    • Provide training on safety protocols and emergency procedures.
  6. Dock Equipment:
    • Set up loading docks with appropriate levelers, dock lights, and signals.
  7. Fire Suppression Systems:
    • Install fire suppression systems, including sprinklers and alarms, to protect against fire hazards.

LED Retrofits

AES offers a comprehensive LED Lighting and Controls Retrofits service, providing businesses with energy-efficient and technologically advanced lighting solutions. This service is designed to enhance illumination, reduce energy consumption, and improve overall lighting control within commercial and industrial spaces. Here's an overview of AES's LED Lighting and Controls Retrofits:

  1. Energy-Efficient LED Lighting:
    • Evaluate existing lighting systems and recommend energy-efficient LED replacements.
    • Install LED fixtures that provide superior illumination while consuming less energy compared to traditional lighting technologies.
  2. Energy Audits and Cost Savings Analysis:
    • Conduct energy audits to assess current energy consumption and identify potential savings through LED retrofits.
    • Provide cost savings analyses to demonstrate the return on investment and long-term financial benefits.
  3. Customized Lighting Designs:
    • Develop customized lighting designs based on the specific needs and layout of each facility.
    • Optimize lighting placement to enhance visibility, comfort, and productivity in different areas of the facility.
  4. Utility Incentives:
    • Identify relevant utility incentive programs available in the specific geographic location where the LED retrofits are planned.
    • Explore opportunities to optimize project designs, control systems, and other factors that can enhance energy savings and qualify for higher incentives.
  5. Controls and Automation Integration:
    • Implement advanced lighting control systems, including occupancy sensors, daylight harvesting, and scheduling.
    • Integrate automation features to adjust lighting levels based on occupancy and natural light availability, maximizing energy savings.
  6. Wireless Controls and Smart Technologies:
    • Utilize wireless control systems for easy installation and flexibility in retrofit projects.
    • Implement smart technologies that allow for remote monitoring, management, and customization of lighting settings.
  7. Dimming and Tunable White Capabilities:
    • Integrate dimming capabilities to adjust lighting levels based on specific requirements or time of day.
    • Provide tunable white lighting options to adjust color temperature, enhancing the visual comfort of occupants.
  8. Occupancy and Daylight Sensors:
    • Install occupancy sensors to automatically turn lights on or off based on the presence of individuals in a space.
    • Implement daylight sensors to adjust artificial lighting levels in response to natural light conditions.
  9. Sustainability and Environmental Impact:
    • Support environmental sustainability by reducing carbon footprint through energy- efficient lighting solutions.
    • Implement eco-friendly practices and disposal methods for old lighting fixtures.
  10. Compliance with Lighting Standards:
    • Ensure that the retrofits comply with relevant lighting standards and codes.
    • Address lighting-related safety and regulatory requirements to meet industry standards.
  11. Project Management and Timely Execution:
    • Manage the entire retrofit project, including planning, procurement, and installation.
    • Ensure timely execution to minimize disruptions to daily operations.
  12. Training and Education Programs:
    • Provide training programs for facility managers and staff to optimize the use of the new lighting and control systems.
    • Offer educational resources to increase awareness of the benefits and features of the upgraded lighting technology.

By offering LED Lighting and Controls Retrofits, AES helps businesses transition to more sustainable and efficient lighting solutions, contributing to cost savings, improved productivity, and a reduced environmental impact. This service aligns with AES's commitment to delivering innovative and turnkey solutions for clients in various industries.

Warehouse Maintenance

As a premier warehouse solution integrator, AES offers a comprehensive Warehouse Maintenance Program to ensure the ongoing functionality, safety, and efficiency of commercial and industrial warehouse facilities. This program encompasses a range of services aimed at addressing maintenance needs and minimizing disruptions to daily operations. Here's an overview of the key elements of AES's Warehouse Maintenance Program:

  1. Scheduled Preventive Maintenance:
    • Develop and implement a proactive schedule for routine inspections and preventive maintenance activities.
    • Address key warehouse components such as racking systems, electrical infrastructure, lighting, and material handling equipment.
    • Racking System Inspection and Optimization:
    • Conduct regular inspections of racking systems to identify and address any damage or potential safety concerns.
    • Provide optimization recommendations to maximize storage capacity and operational efficiency.
  2. Electrical Infrastructure Maintenance:
    • Ensure the integrity and reliability of the electrical infrastructure supporting warehouse operations.
    • Address issues related to power distribution, lighting systems, and electrical equipment.
  3. Lighting System Maintenance and Upgrades:
    • Implement a lighting maintenance program to ensure consistent illumination throughout the warehouse.
    • Propose LED lighting upgrades for energy efficiency and improved visibility.
  4. Material Handling Equipment Inspection and Maintenance:
    • Perform regular inspections and maintenance on material handling equipment, such as forklifts and conveyors.
    • Address issues promptly to minimize downtime and extend equipment lifespan.
  5. Emergency Preparedness and Safety Checks:
    • Conduct safety inspections and ensure compliance with safety regulations and codes.
    • Implement emergency preparedness measures, including egress lighting and fire suppression system checks.
  6. Dock Equipment Inspection and Maintenance:
    • Regularly inspect and maintain dock equipment, including levelers, dock lights, and signals.
    • Ensure smooth and secure loading and unloading operations.
  7. Compliance with Authorities Having Jurisdiction (AHJ):
    • Stay informed about and comply with regulations set by local authorities and regulatory bodies.
    • Facilitate the necessary documentation and permits to meet AHJ requirements.
  8. Training and Education Programs:
    • Provide training programs for warehouse staff on safety protocols, equipment operation, and emergency procedures.
    • Offer educational resources to enhance the knowledge and awareness of maintenance practices.
  9. Customized Maintenance Plans:
    • Tailor maintenance plans based on the specific needs and operations of each warehouse facility.
    • Work collaboratively with clients to prioritize maintenance activities based on critical factors.
  10. Responsive Support and Emergency Services:
    • Establish a responsive support system for addressing urgent maintenance issues and emergencies.
    • Provide timely and efficient solutions to minimize disruptions to warehouse operations.
  11. Technology Integration for Maintenance Management:
    • Utilize advanced technologies for maintenance management, including predictive maintenance tools and real-time monitoring systems.
    • Streamline communication and reporting through digital platforms.

Through AES's Warehouse Maintenance Program, clients benefit from a proactive and strategic approach to facility upkeep, ensuring a safe, efficient, and well-maintained warehouse environment. This program aligns with AES's commitment to delivering turnkey solutions and maintaining invaluable partnerships with clients.
