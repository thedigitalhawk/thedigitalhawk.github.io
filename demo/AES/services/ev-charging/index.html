EV Charging

As a premier solutions integrator, AES extends its expertise to Electric Vehicle (EV) charging installation for commercial customers, offering a comprehensive and tailored approach to meet the specific needs of businesses.

Here's an outline of AES's approach to EV charging installation:

  1. Site Evaluation and Planning:
    • Conduct a thorough site assessment to identify optimal locations for EV charging stations.
    • Collaborate to understand parking patterns, site accessibility, and electrical infrastructure.
  2. Charging Station Selection and Design:
    • Assist in choosing suitable charging station models based on the commercial site's requirements and expected usage patterns.
    • Design an efficient layout for charging stations to optimize parking spaces and user accessibility.
  3. Electrical Infrastructure Enhancement:
    • Assess the current electrical capacity of the site and provide recommendations for any necessary upgrades.
    • Work with the customer to ensure the electrical infrastructure supports the additional load from EV charging.
  4. Permitting and Compliance:
    • Manage the permitting process, obtaining all necessary approvals from local authorities and utilities.
    • Ensure compliance with building codes, zoning regulations, and any specific requirements for EV charging installations.
  5. Advanced Networking and Management Systems:
    • Implement state-of-the-art networked charging solutions for seamless monitoring and management of charging stations.
    • Integrate management systems to track usage, perform billing, and streamline maintenance processes.
  6. Installation Logistics and Timing:
    • Plan and execute the installation with minimal disruption to regular business operations.
    • Coordinate installation schedules to ensure timely completion while considering the commercial customer's needs.
  7. Payment and Access Solutions:
    • Integrate user-friendly payment systems for easy access to charging stations, supporting various payment methods.
    • Consider implementing secure access control systems compatible with existing security measures.
  8. Scalability and Futureproofing:
    • Design installations with scalability in mind, allowing for future expansion as demand for EV charging grows.
    • Stay informed about evolving industry standards and technologies to ensure the infrastructure remains cutting-edge.
  9. Employee and Customer Engagement Programs:
    • Develop educational materials and engagement programs to inform employees and customers about the benefits of on-site EV charging.
    • Highlight any incentives or perks associated with the use of EV charging stations.
  10. Maintenance and Support Services:
    • Establish a proactive maintenance plan for regular inspections and timely repairs.
    • Provide a user-friendly support system for reporting issues and seeking assistance.
  11. Regulatory Incentives and Partnerships:
    • Explore and capture available regulatory incentives, grants, or partnerships that may benefit commercial customers investing in EV charging infrastructure.
    • Leverage AES's industry connections to facilitate access to relevant incentives.

By leveraging AES's experience and commitment to efficient and sustainable solutions, the EV charging installation for commercial customers becomes a streamlined and future-ready process, contributing to the broader goals of environmental responsibility and technological advancement.
